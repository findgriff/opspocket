FAQ

Common questions.

When does Cloud launch?

Right now we're onboarding the waitlist. Join and we'll email you when your tier is ready (expect a few weeks). Early members get locked-in founding pricing.

Can I bring my own OpenAI / Anthropic keys?

Yes — plug in your own API keys at any time. Or use our included Ollama option for local inference with no external API costs. Your keys are stored encrypted and never leave your VPS.

What happens if I cancel?

Cancel monthly any time — your VPS is retained for 7 days then decommissioned. Annual plans can be cancelled at renewal; mid-term refunds are pro-rated for the months you haven't used.

Can I upgrade or downgrade tiers?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at your next billing cycle. We migrate your data automatically.

Where is my data hosted?

Hetzner Cloud, EU (Falkenstein or Nuremberg, Germany) by default. Agency tier can request specific Hetzner regions on request.

How does the Starter annual "free app" bundle work?

Pay £176.59 for a full year of Cloud Starter and we include the iOS app (normally £8.50/mo) at no extra cost — a £102 saving. Monthly Starter customers can add the app separately.

Do I need to know Docker / SSH?

No. That's the point. You get one URL and one password. The mobile app and web UI are what you interact with.

Who's behind OpsPocket?

OpsPocket is a small, UK-based indie shop. We build the app, run the cloud, answer your support emails. Not a VC-backed platform — no surprise pivots.
